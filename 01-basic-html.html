How to Make Homemade Ice-Cream With a Bag

Only takes 15 minutes, but yields one serving.

For this recipe, you will need:

  • 1 cup half and half
  • 2 tbsp. of grandualted sugar
  • 1/2 tbsp. of vanilla extract
  • 3 cups of ice
  • 1/3 c. of kosher salt
  • any toppings of your choice
  • a small ziploc bag
  • a large ziploc bag

Steps:

  1. In the small ziploc bag, pour half-and-half, sugar, and vanilla extract. Push out excess air and seal it. Make sure
  2. Into the large ziploc bag, combine ice and salt.
  3. Place small bag inside the bigger bag and shake vigorously for about 7 to 10 minutes until ice cream has hardened.
  4. Remove from bag and enjoy with your favorite ice cream toppings.
